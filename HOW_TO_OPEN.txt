MISANTHROPIC SITE - HOW TO OPEN (EASY INSTRUCTIONS)

1. Open the Terminal app (Cmd + Space, type "Terminal", press Enter).

2. Paste the following command and press Enter:

   cd ~/Desktop/misanthropic-site
   bash "START SITE.command"

   (This starts the server and tries to open the browser for you)

3. When you see "Serving HTTP on ..." in the Terminal, the server is running.

4. If the browser does NOT open or you get "this site can't be reached":

   - Click the address bar at the VERY TOP of your browser (Chrome/Safari).
     (NOT the Google search box! The long bar at the top of the page.)

   - Delete everything in it.

   - Paste exactly this:

     http://127.0.0.1:8765

   - Press Enter.

   Alternative:
     http://localhost:8765

   NOTE: Just typing "localhost" will not work. You must include the port (:8765).

5. Once the site loads, scroll down. The game, quiz, stats etc. will be visible.

6. To close the site:
   - Press Enter in the Terminal window (the script will stop the server).
   - Or press Ctrl + C.
